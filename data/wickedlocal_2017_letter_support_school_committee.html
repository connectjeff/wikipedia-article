NATICK LETTER: Support for School Committee candidate Jeff Alderson

Staff Writer
Wicked Local
March 23, 2017, 1:09 p.m. ET

I am writing to recommend Jeff Alderson for a position on the Natick School Committee. I am the Co-founder of Waypoint Adventure, a non-profit organization that leads adventure education opportunities for youth and adults with disabilities. Jeff has been a board member of Waypoint’s Board of Directors since 2014 - initially serving as Treasurer. He stepped up to Chair the Board in January of 2016. Jeff and his wife Sara have been volunteers on programs since 2013 and are lead investors in Waypoint’s mission.

Jeff interacts well with people of all abilities, ages, races and genders. Jeff is a leader and a team player. He is willing to step out of his comfort zone and support others while encouraging them to do the same. Jeff is multi-talented and is willing to lend his expertise in support of a variety of causes. In addition to volunteering on adventure programs, Jeff has helped Waypoint create and manage our database and acted as our program photographer. Each year since joining the board, Jeff has helped by asking critical questions around our core mission/vision. He ensures that governance is the primary role of the board and keeps the board from getting bogged down in management issues. He has a sharp eye when it is time to work out our yearly budget and asks tough questions. Jeff is a hard worker and rolls up his sleeves to get the job done.

As someone who has seen Jeff in the roles described above, I can highly recommend Jeff for a position on the Natick School Committee. And, I strongly encourage Natick residents to vote for Jeff Alderson for Natick School Committee on March 28th!

Dan Minnich

Bolton
