Data Summits focus on community development, 
implementation, integration, maintenance and exchange 
of data and data standards.  Access, data quality, overall 
connectivity, and political factors that drive information 
systems and interoperability are also discussed. 
 
All Summit meetings and events are open to all 
registered attendees.  Transparent collaboration, 
engaging discussions, awareness of technical resources 
and best practices, identification of emerging 
technologies, new business contacts, and tips from 
experts of leading community organizations can be 
expected. 
 
Register today! 
 
Agenda 
 
In continuing its mission of transparent, community-based collaboration, 
the Spring 2011 Data Summit is held in partnership with the AACRAO 
SPEEDE Committee, with the Common Education Data Standards (CEDS) 
Initiative and Consortium, IMS, SIF Association, the Data Quality 
Campaign (DQC) and the US Department of Education.  The Spring 2011 
Data Summit includes PESC’s 13th Annual Membership Meeting and 
Annual Spring Reception.

CONCURRENT WORKING AND GENERAL SESSION DESCRIPTIONS 
 
ACADEMIC E-PORTFOLIO 
A current PESC development workgroup within the Standards Forum for Education focusing on  the development of 
an XML standard to support ePortfolio stakeholders. 
Co-Chairs: John Ittelson, Professor Emeritus, California State University Monterey Bay  
 ittelson@csumb.edu 
 Don Phillips, Xap Corporation 
  don.phillips@xap.com  
 
COMMON EDUCATION DATA STANDARDS (CONCURRENT) 
An informal discussion focusing on current and future activities of CEDS.  
Co-Chairs: Hans L’Orange, Vice President, SHEEO  
 hans@sheeo.org 
 Alex Jackl  
 ajackl@gmail.com 
 
ELECTRONIC AUTHENTICATION/AUTHORIZATION (EA2) TASK FORCE 
A current PESC task force focusing on advancement, yet simplification of user experiences with education 
technology and resources, support of portability of data and research, maximization of  the value and integrity of 
education technology all in a secured and trustworthy environment.  
Co-Chairs: Charlie Leonhardt, Principal Technologist, Georgetown University  
 leonhardt@georgetown.edu 
Arnie Miles, Middleware Architect & Assistant Adjunct Professor of Computer Sciences, Georgetown 
University  
 adm35@georgetown.edu 
 
EDUNIFY TASK FORCE 
A current PESC task force focusing on web services, service oriented architecture and public/private registries. 
Co-Chairs: Steve Wheat, Chief IT Architect, Emory University  
 swheat@emory.edu  
 Jim Wager, Vice President, SCRIP-SAFE International  
 jwager@scrip-safe.com  
 
EDUCATION RECORD USER GROUP 
A current PESC user group within the Standards Forum for Education focusing on  maintenance of approved EDI & 
XML standards related to the admissions and registrar functions  (high school & college transcripts, admissions 
application, test scores, PDFs, etc.) 
Co-Chairs: Tuan Ahn Do, San Francisco State University  
 doey@sfsu.edu  
 Bob Hewett, Pearson  
 bhewett@pearson.com  
 
TECHNICAL ADVISORY BOARD (TAB) 
A current PESC committee within the Standards Forum for Education  that supports all development and directs the 
strategic direction of technical issues. 
Co-Chairs: Michael Morris, Director, ACT  
 michael.morris@act.org 
 Gideon Sanstra, SunGard Higher Education  
 gideon.sanstra@sungardhe.com  
 
CHANGE CONTROL BOARD (CCB) 
A current PESC committee within the Standards Forum for Education  that authorizes the approval of new 
standards and approved maintenance of existing standards. 
Chair: Kristi Blabaum, Great Lakes Educational Loan Services  
 kblabaum@glhec.org  
 
SEAL OF APPROVAL BOARD (SAB) 
A current PESC committee focusing on launching a new service within PESC that will ensure uniform 
implementation of PESC Approved Standards. 
Chair: Francisco Valines, Director of Financial Aid, Florida International University  
 valinesf@fiu.edu
