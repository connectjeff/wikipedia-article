ICYMI: Who's Running In The 2017 Natick Town Election?

Natick's annual town election is March 28 this year.

Charlene Arsenault, Patch Staff
Posted Sat, Mar 11, 2017 at 9:00 am ET

NATICK, MA — Natick voters head to the polls this year on Tuesday, March 28, for the annual town election, casting their votes in several categories for town positions.

This year, there are four contested races, for Board of Selectmen, Constable, School Committee and Recreation and Parks Commission.

Polls are open 7 a.m. to 8 p.m.
