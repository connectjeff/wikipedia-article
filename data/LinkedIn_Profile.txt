Summary
I’m an education technology leader with more than 25 years of
experience spanning product strategy, systems architecture,
interoperability standards, instructional technology, and public-sector
governance. At MathWorks, I lead education product marketing
initiatives focused on teaching and learning workflows, including
MATLAB Grader and MATLAB Course Designer—platforms used
by thousands of instructors and hundreds of thousands of learners
worldwide. My current work includes the application of generative
and agentic AI to improve instructional design, assessment, and
hands-on technical learning experiences. My background combines
technical systems leadership with deep experience in higher
education, standards development, and organizational process
design. Across roles at MathWorks, Eduventures, Houghton
Mifflin Harcourt, ConnectEDU, Oracle, and the U.S. Air Force, I
have worked at the intersection of technology, education, data
interoperability, and large-scale operational systems. Outside of my
professional work, I serve as the elected Town Moderator for the
Town of Natick, Massachusetts, where I preside over Representative
Town Meeting and support transparent, effective civic governance.
I have also served on nonprofit and educational boards focused on
accessibility, disability inclusion, and educational opportunity. Areas
of interest include AI-enabled learning, systems thinking, educational
interoperability, civic process modernization, accessibility, and
organizational leadership.
