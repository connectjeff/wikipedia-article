Dear Mr. Mullins:
On behalf of the Board of Directors of the Postsecondary Electronic Standards
Council (PESC), I am very pleased to notify you that South Carolina has been
awarded WINNER of PESC’s 12th Annual Best Practices Competition. Our Board
Review Committee was very pleased with the "South Carolina Transfer &
Articulation Center (SC TRAC)” submission and by unanimous agreement, found
it worthy of this distinction and award.
An Awards Ceremony is planned during PESC’s upcoming Spring 2011 Data
Summit April 26-28, 2011 in Boston. We have allocated time on the Summit
agenda for you to present your winning submission. We hope you are able to
attend in person to receive the award and make a presentation. The submission
will also be posted prominently on the Best Practices page of PESC's website
along with all previous award winners.
Many thanks to you and the entire team in South Carolina for providing
valuable information and services to the education community, and
once again, congratulations!
My best regards
