Ensure Student Success with ProctorFree
ProctorFree will be attending
Eduventures Summit 2016
in Boston, MA from October 23-25, 2016.
ProctorFree is excited to connect with other higher education leaders and industry trailblazers and rethink the student lifecycle at Eduventures Summit 2016. Our vision for the future of the student lifecycle is one where online students succeed with the help of online proctoring solutions.
Keynote presentations at Eduventures Summit 2016 will focus on higher education topics in which online proctoring has a crucial role. Online proctoring improves the online student testing experience and helps retain the growing population of nontraditional students by making an online education convenient and accessible for all students.
All of the presentations are sure to be informative and thought-provoking. We are particularly excited about the Keynote presentation, “Technology that Should be on Every Higher Education Leader’s Radar,” by Jeff Alderson, Principal Analyst at Eduventures. Attend Alderson’s presentation on Monday, October 24, from 1:30 PM to 2:15 PM. Learn why ProctorFree is a company to watch in higher education and edtech in the coming year.
Prior to the upcoming presentation at Eduventures Summit 2016, Eduventures had recognized ProctorFree’s commitment to continuous improvement of the quality and effectiveness of offered products. Alderson
recently stated
that, “ProctorFree is so committed to improving the quality of their algorithm they are now auditing 100% of their automated exam sessions using real people. This has the effect of proctoring every session twice – not an environment where students should press their luck by testing the system.”
If you want to learn more about how ProctorFree ensures academic integrity while maintaining an exceptional test taking experience for students, connect with us at Eduventures Summit 2016.
Contact us
to set up a meeting or to schedule a personal demonstration.
Rethink the student lifecycle with online proctoring at
Eduventures Summit 2016
. The future of student success is bright with ProctorFree.
