## Submission Steps

1. Create or log in to a Wikipedia account.
2. Add a clear COI disclosure on your user page or the draft talk page before submission.
3. Create the draft at `Draft:Jeffrey Alderson`.
4. Paste the contents of `drafts/article_draft.wikitext`.
5. Keep `{{subst:submit}}` at the top only when ready to submit for AfC review.
6. In the submission comment, state that you are the subject and have a COI.
7. Do not add the article directly to mainspace yourself.

## Policy Notes Checked

- Articles for Creation is the appropriate route for editors with a conflict of interest.
- Biographies of living persons require careful sourcing, neutral tone, and removal of contentious or poorly sourced material.
- Standalone biography notability generally depends on significant coverage in reliable secondary sources independent of the subject.
