Scope: Wicked Local sources reviewed for the Jeffrey Alderson AfC proposal.

## Bottom Line

The Wicked Local sources are useful for the 2017 Natick School Committee campaign. One is a letter of support, which is opinion and should not be used for notability. The other is a candidate-question article that includes Alderson's candidate response alongside other School Committee candidates. It verifies campaign participation and policy positions, but remains local election coverage rather than biographical coverage.
