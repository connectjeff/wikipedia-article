# PESC Source Review for Jeffrey Alderson

Scope: PESC sources reviewed for the Jeffrey Alderson AfC proposal.

## Bottom Line

The PESC sources verify several narrow professional-context facts: Alderson was listed as a PESC Board of Directors member while affiliated with ConnectEDU; appeared in PESC program materials with former Massachusetts governor Jane Swift, also then listed with ConnectEDU; co-presented in a PESC standards session at the 2011 NCES STATS-DC conference; presented on MyDataButton at the 2012 Data Summit; was listed in the NCES MIS 2013 MyDataButton session with PESC and eScholar representatives; participated in a PESC Education Record User Group call about MyData; presented Eduventures portfolio-vendor research at the 2016 PESC Fall Data Symposium & Summit; and was listed as an Eduventures contributor to the Academic Credential & Experiential Learning implementation guide. CEDS context sources are tracked separately in `sources/ceds_sources.md`. These are organizational or hosted program/call-note sources, so they are useful for verification but weak for standalone notability.
