# Campus Technology Source Review for Jeffrey Alderson

Scope: Campus Technology sources reviewed for the Jeffrey Alderson AfC proposal.

## Bottom Line

The Campus Technology articles are useful independent trade-publication sources for Alderson's education technology career and related project context. The 2016 articles identify him as an Eduventures principal analyst and use him as a source on learning management systems and higher-education analytics. The 2007 ConnectEDU article does not name Alderson, but it independently documents the transcript-exchange and college-planning platform context that overlaps with other ConnectEDU/PESC evidence. These sources support professional context, but none is a full biographical profile.
