# Campus Technology Source Review for Jeffrey Alderson

Scope: Campus Technology sources reviewed for the Jeffrey Alderson AfC proposal.

## Bottom Line

The Campus Technology articles are useful independent trade-publication sources for Alderson's education technology career. They identify him as an Eduventures principal analyst and use him as a source on learning management systems and higher-education analytics. They support professional context, but neither is a full biographical profile.
