Verified details:

- Title: `The Legacy of inBloom`
- Authors: Monica Bulger, Patrick McCormick, Mikaela Pitcan
- Date: Working Paper 02.02.2017
- The report identifies Jeff Alderson as former Lead Architect at Houghton Mifflin Harcourt, formerly Choice Solutions, and as the technical architect for the New York implementation of inBloom.
- Appendix 1 lists Alderson's role during inBloom as Chief Solutions Architect at ConnectEDU and Lead Architect at Houghton Mifflin Harcourt, and his then-current role as Principal Analyst of Enterprise Software at Eduventures.
