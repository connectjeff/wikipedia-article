Scope: affiliated, corporate, self-published, and profile-like sources reviewed or retained as leads.

## Bottom Line

These sources are generally weak for AfC notability because they are affiliated with Alderson, employer-related, self-published, or too sparse. They may help identify facts to verify elsewhere, but they should not carry the article's notability argument.
