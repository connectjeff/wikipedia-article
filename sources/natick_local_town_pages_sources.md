## Bottom Line

The March 2017 Natick Local Town Pages issue is useful local election coverage. It identifies Alderson, Donna McKenzie, and Hayley Sonneborn as candidates for two three-year Natick School Committee seats and includes candidate-submitted statements. Because the Alderson material is candidate-authored, it should be used cautiously and not as independent notability support.
