# Town of Natick Source Review for Jeffrey Alderson

Scope: Town of Natick official website sources reviewed for the Jeffrey Alderson AfC proposal.

## Bottom Line

The Town of Natick election-results PDF is an official primary source for the March 28, 2017 Annual Town Election. It verifies the School Committee result: Hayley I. Sonneborn received 1,402 votes, Donna M. Mckenzie received 1,020 votes, and Jeffrey A. Alderson received 1,012 votes. The official margin between McKenzie and Alderson was 8 votes. The Town's Hybrid Town Meeting Committee page verifies the committee as an official board/committee page and provides public survey context, but it does not name Alderson; use it only to corroborate the committee's official existence and scope alongside Natick Report's operational coverage of Alderson's action as moderator.
