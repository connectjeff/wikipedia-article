# A Cappella and Overboard Source Review

Scope: source notes for Jeff Alderson's documented a cappella activity, including Overboard and Harmony Sweepstakes records.

## Bottom Line

Harmony Sweepstakes records directly verify that Alderson was listed as a member of Overboard for the 2008 Boston Regional, where Overboard placed third. The same records list Alderson with Integration by Parts / Integration By Parts in the 2003, 2004, and 2005 Boston Regionals, including a 2003 Best Original Arrangement award for the group. The cited Wikipedia article verifies general Overboard background and notes that Overboard won the 2011 Boston Regional Harmony Sweepstakes, but the 2011 Harmony Sweepstakes roster for Overboard lists Scott Cobban, Jeff Eames, Nick Girard, Alfredo Austin III, and Caleb Whelden, not Jeff Alderson.

Assessment: useful as light biographical/context material and source leads for music activity, but not a strong AfC notability source for Alderson. The sources are event/competition records and a Wikipedia article about the group, not independent biographical coverage of Alderson. Do not use the Wikipedia article itself as an article citation; if this fact is added to the draft, cite the underlying Harmony Sweepstakes pages and keep the wording narrow.

## Verified Details

- The Harmony Sweepstakes person page title is `Jeff Alderson A Cappella Singer`.
- The person page lists a 2008 Overboard entry and states `Boston Harmony Sweepstakes 3rd Place Winners`.
- The Overboard 2008 detail page states `2008 Boston 3rd place` and lists members including `Jeff Alderson`.
- The 2008 Boston Regional page lists the results as Syncopation first, 5-Alone second, and Overboard third; its Overboard section lists `Jeff Alderson` among members.
- The Wikipedia Overboard article and 2011 Boston Regional page state that Overboard won the 2011 Boston Regional Harmony Sweepstakes.
- The 2011 Boston Regional roster and 2011 National Finals roster for Overboard do not list Alderson; they list Jeff Eames among the members, which should not be confused with Jeff Alderson.
- The Harmony Sweepstakes person page separately lists Alderson with Integration By Parts / Integration by Parts in 2003, 2004, and 2005.
- The 2003 Integration by Parts detail page lists `2003 Boston Best Original Arrangement` and includes Alderson in the member list.
