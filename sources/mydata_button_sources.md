# MyDataButton Source Review for Jeffrey Alderson

Scope: sources reviewed for background on the White House / U.S. Department of Education MyDataButton initiative discussed in PESC sources.

## Bottom Line

The reviewed MyDataButton sources verify broader context for the initiative. Hack Education is a contemporary independent education-technology source that describes the proposed MyData button and names Houghton Mifflin Harcourt among industry players on board with the initiative. The ESP Solutions Group paper later critiques the initiative and says the White House, Secretary of Education, and several companies promoted an education-data download button in 2012. Neither source mentions Alderson, ConnectEDU, or PESC directly. Use them only as background context for the initiative; keep Alderson-specific claims tied to the PESC Spring 2012 program and ERUG call notes.
