# MyDataButton Source Review for Jeffrey Alderson

Scope: sources reviewed for background on the White House / U.S. Department of Education MyDataButton initiative discussed in PESC sources.

## Bottom Line

The NCES MIS 2013 agenda directly connects Alderson to the MyDataButton initiative in a government conference program. It lists the session `IX-F MyDataButton` with Michael Sessa of PESC, Jeffrey Alderson of ConnectEDU, Inc., and Shawn Bay of eScholar LLC; the session description says the White House and U.S. Department of Education teamed up on MyDataButton and that PESC representatives had participated in the initiative. A February 2013 PESC publication hosted on Yumpu separately lists `JEFFREY ALDERSON, Senior Director of Product Innovation, ConnectEDU - MyData`.

The other reviewed MyDataButton sources verify broader context for the initiative. Hack Education is a contemporary independent education-technology source that describes the proposed MyData button and names Houghton Mifflin Harcourt among industry players on board with the initiative. The ESP Solutions Group paper later critiques the initiative and says the White House, Secretary of Education, and several companies promoted an education-data download button in 2012. Use these background sources only as context; keep Alderson-specific claims tied to NCES2013, PESC2012, PESCNEWS2013, and ERUG1.
